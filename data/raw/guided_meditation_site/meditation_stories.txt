Thousands of people come to The Guided Meditation Site every month looking for information about meditation. We do our best to explain thebenefits of meditationto them, but we could never do this as well as YOU can.In fact, we can't think of a better way to illustrate the benefits of meditation to everyone than by sharing YOUR meditation story with them.So if you meditate, and if meditation has brought you peace, now you have the chance to bring that kind of peace to others.Your words have the power to inspire and inform other people who are interested in meditation, so we wholeheartedly encourage you to take part.

Click here to share your meditation story now...

There is no right or wrong way to tell your story. There's no minimum or maximum word limit, and you don't have to write like Shakespeare. Just tell your story in whatever way comes naturally to you.

We believe that the simple truth of a personal story is the most powerful and the mostrealway to show other people what meditation is all about.Your story might inspire someone to start meditating, and who knows where the benefits of that will lead? Your words may heal a broken heart, they might heal a disease, they might restore a relationship or perhaps they will finally bring peace to a troubled soul.So if meditation has helped YOU find peace, then why not help others find peace.Tell your own meditation storyand enjoy the reward of gratitude, a sense of fulfillment and joy that comes from knowing that you are helping others.

If you are new to meditation, then please explore the stories that our dear visitors have written for you!

Tell uswhyyou meditate. What do you get out of it?How did you get started with meditation and what prompted you to start?How has meditation changed you? Has it improved your life in any way?What do you think life would be likewithoutmeditation?Would you recommend meditation to others?
